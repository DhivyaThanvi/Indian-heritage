Sikkim

Sikkim


Chief Minister-Prem Singh Tamang

Prem Singh Tamang (born 5 February 1968), better known as P. S. Golay, is an Indian politician, and former teacher who is serving as the 6th and current Chief Minister of Sikkim since 2019 and the founder and leader of the Sikkim Krantikari Morcha since 2019.

Governor-Om Prakash Mathur

Om Prakash Mathur (born 2 January 1952) is an Indian politician currently serving as the Governor of Sikkim. He is a former member of Rajya Sabha from Rajasthan state in India and is a senior politician from Bharatiya Janata Party.

Culture and Heritage


Food
Blend of Nepalese

Sikkim's cuisine is a blend of Tibetan, Nepali, and native Sikkimese dishes, and is influenced by the region's many ethnic groups. Here are some traditional foods of Sikkim that are influenced by Nepalese cuisine.

Agriculture
Orange

Sikkim's most important commercial fruit is the Sikkim Mandarin orange, which is a native fruit of the state. It's similar to the Assam, Darjeeling, and Nepal mandarins.

Festival
Losoong

The Losoong festival is celebrated on the occasion of the end of the harvest season and the end of the tenth month of the Tibetan Year in the rural Sikkim. On this festival, the Chaam dances are performed at the Tsu-La-Khang monastery, Phodong monastery and Rumtek monastery.

Dress
Bakhu,Kho

The traditional dress of Sikkim includes the Bakhu, a long, loose dress worn by both men and women, and the Kho, a robe worn by Bhutia men and women.

Music
Tamang Selo

A traditional folk dance and music style that is performed by the Tamang community. The dance is accompanied by the Dhampu, a traditional musical instrument. The music is used to express emotions and tell folklore to children.

Dance
Mask dance

A popular dance that involves performers wearing masks and costumes to celebrate life events and pay homage to sacred places and teachers.

Architecture
Lepcha houses

Also known as "Bamboo Houses" or "Stilt Houses", these houses are built on stilts made of bamboo or wood, and have evolved over generations to address the region's climate and geography.
