Madhya Pradesh

Madhya Pradesh


Chief Minister-Mohan Yadav

Dr. Mohan Yadav is an Indian politician and businessman serving as the 19th and Current Chief Minister of Madhya Pradesh since 2023.

Governor-Mangubhai C. Patel

Mangubhai Chhaganbhai Patel is an Indian statesman who is the current and 19th Governor of Madhya Pradesh. He is a leader of the Bharatiya Janata Party from Gujarat.

Culture and Heritage


Food
Bhopali Gosht Korma

Bhopali Gosht Korma is a rich and aromatic non-vegetarian dish from Madhya Pradesh that's often served at celebrations and parties. It's a slow-cooked mutton dish with a creamy gravy made with yogurt, cream, and a blend of spices.

Agriculture
Pulses

Major pulses produce in the state are- Arhar, Gram, Urad, Moong, and lentil. Pulses in Madhya Pradesh are sown in both Rabi and Kharif seasons..

Festival
Lokrang Festival

A cultural festival that showcases folk dances, music, and handicrafts from the tribal community of Madhya Pradesh.

Dress
Dhoti Lehenga choli

Dhoti is a traditional clothing item for men in Madhya Pradesh.Lehenga choli is a popular traditional dress for women in Madhya Pradesh.

Music
Bundelkhand

The Bundelkhand region is known for its folk music and dance, which are based on local traditions, seasons, and nature. Each month has its own folk music and dance.

Dance
Jawara

Jawara, a harvest dance, is usually performed in the Bundelkhand area of Madhya Pradesh. It reflects the gaiety and pleasure of the peasants who have attained a good harvest.

Architecture
Sanchi stupa

Sanchi stupa is a Buddhist complex, famous for its Greagt Stupa, on a hilltop at Sanchi ztown in Raisen District of the state of Madhya Pradesh, Located 46 km. north east of Bhopal, the capital city of Madhya Pradesh, India.
