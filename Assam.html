Assam

Assam


Chief Minister-Himanta Biswa Sarma

Himanta Biswa Sarma is an Indian politician and lawyer serving as the 15th and current Chief Minister of Assam since 2021. A former member of the Indian National Congress, Sarma joined the Bharatiya Janata Party on 23 August 2015 and soon was made convenor of NEDA.

Governor- Lakshman Acharya

Lakshman Prasad Acharya is the 32nd Governor of Assam. Chief Justice Gauhati High Court Hon'ble Mr. Justice Vijay Bishnoi administered the oath of office to the new Governor. Shri Lakshman Prasad Acharya hails from Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh.

Culture and Heritage


Food
Fish

Fish dishes: Fish is a staple of Assamese cuisine and is prepared in many ways, including fish curry, fish fry,fish roasted in banana leaves, and fish tarts.

Agriculture
Green manure crop Dhaincha

Green manure crop Dhaincha (Local name-khori goch) is mainly grown as fencing crop which serves as a soil addi- tive. In situ manuring of rice fields, such as the practice of allowing the animals to graze directly on fields is very common.

Festival
Bihu

Bihu is the most important festival of Assam. It is celebrated with joy and abundance by all Assamese people irrespective of caste, creed, religion, faith and belief.

Dress
Mekhela Chador- Fotua and Eri Chaddar.

Mekhela Sador is a traditional Assamese sarong traditionally worn by Assamese women. There are two main pieces of cloth that are draped around the bodyAssam state has the traditional dress of men known as “Sola or Fotua” and “Eri Chaddar”. The Gamocha also is known as bihuwaans is a hand-woven cloth, is the symbol of Assam weaving craft.

Music
Bihu

Famous songs that are sung to the pentatonic scale and often feature themes like love, the Assamese new year, and farmers' lives. Bihu music is played with instruments like the dhol, mohor singor pepe, cymbal, gogona (bamboo flute), and toka (bamboo clapper).

Dance
Bihu

The Bihu dance is a traditional folk dance of Assam, India, that is an important part of the Bihu festival and Assamese culture. The dance is characterized by quick steps and hand movements, and is often performed by young men and women in groups.

Architecture
Beton

Assam-type architecture also known as "Beton" or "Baton" is an architectural style developed in the state of Assam in India during the late modern period. It is found in Assam and Sylhet region. The houses constructed using this style are generally termed as Assam-type houses, consisting usually one or more storeys.
