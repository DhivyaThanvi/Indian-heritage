Karnataka

Karnataka


Chief Minister-Siddaramaiah

Chief Minister Sri Siddaramaiah. I was born on August 03, 1948, in Siddaramana Hundi of Varuna Hobli in Mysore district. My family was mainly dependent on agriculture.

Governor-Thawar Chand Gehlot

Thawar Chand Gehlot is an Indian politician who is the current and 19th Governor of Karnataka, being the first person serving as the Governor of Karnataka from Madhya Pradesh.

Culture and Heritage


Food
Mysore Pak

Mysore Pak is a popular sweet from Karnataka made primarily from ghee and gram flour. The term 'Pak' originated from Paaka, which refers to the sugary syrup used as sweetener in Mysore Pak.

Agriculture
wheat

In financial year 2022, about 212 thousand metric tons of wheat was produced in the southern state of Karnataka. This was a decrease from the previous fiscal year. Wheat is one of the main cereal crops grown in the country.

Festival
Ugadi

Ugadi is a festival celebrated in Karnataka that marks the beginning of the New Year and spring. It's celebrated on the first day of Chaitra on the Hindu lunisolar calendar, which usually falls in the second half of March or first half of April.

Dress
Kurta,Lungi sarees

The traditional dress for men includes the Kurta, Lungi, and Angavastram, while women wear sarees, silk skirts with tops, and Ilkal sarees.

Music
Carnatic music

A form of Indian classical music that originated in South India and is based on ancient Hindu traditions. Carnatic music is characterized by vocal harmony, and instruments are played to imitate singing.

Dance
Yakshagana

A popular dance-drama form that combines dance, music, singing, and dialogue. It's a fusion of folk and classical traditions, and is performed in colorful costumes. The word "Yakshagana" means "celestial music". It originated in the royal courts of the Vijayanagara empire.

Architecture
Badami Chalukya architecture

The Chalukya empire's capital, Badami, is known for its Chalukyan style of temple architecture that developed between the 5th and 8th centuries.
