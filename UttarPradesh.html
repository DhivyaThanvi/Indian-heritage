Uttar Pradesh


Chief Minister-Yogi Adityanath

Yogi Adityanath (born Ajay Mohan Singh Bisht; 5 June 1972) is an Indian Hindu monk and politician, belonging to the Bharatiya Janata Party who has been serving as the Chief Minister of Uttar Pradesh since 19 March 2017.

Governor-Anandiben Patel

Anandiben Mafatbhai Patel (born 21 November 1941) is an Indian politician serving as the 20th and current Governor of Uttar Pradesh. She also served as 17th Governor of Madhya Pradesh and Governor of Chhattisgarh.

Culture and Heritage


Food
Awadhi Biryani

Awadhi Biryani is a traditional food of Uttar Pradesh, India. It's also known as Lucknowi Biryani and is famous for its delicate flavors and tender meat.

Festival
Ramlila

The Ramlila is a series of plays that tell the story of Rama's life, from his birth to his marriage, exile, and killing of Ravana. The performance includes song, narration, dialogue, and dance.

Music
khayal

khayal, in Hindustani music, a musical form based on a Hindi song in two parts that recur between expanding cycles of melodic and rhythmic improvisation. In a standard performance a slow (vilambit) khayal is followed by a shorter, fast (drut) khayal in the same raga (melodic framework). Also spelled: khyal or kayal.

Dance
Raslila

Raslila is a traditional folk dance drama from Uttar Pradesh that is based on the life of Krishna. It is a theatrical dance form that is also known as the "love dance".
