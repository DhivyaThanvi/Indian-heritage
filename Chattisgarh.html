Chattisgarh

chattisgarh


Chief Minister-Vishnu Deo Sai

Vishnu Deo Sai is an Indian politician currently serving as the 4th Chief Minister of Chhattisgarh. He is the first senior tribal leader in Central India. Sai is the member of Bharatiya Janata Party and Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh.

Governor-Ramen Deka

Ramen Deka is an Indian politician currently serving as the 8th Governor of Chattisgarh. He was a member of Bharatiya Janata Party. He was a Member of Parliament, Lok Sabha in the 16th Lok Sabha and represented Mangaldoi.

Culture and Heritage


Food
Tilgur

Tilgur of Til k Ladoo is a famous dish that has got it origin from Chhattisgarh state. The roasted sesame seeds are mixed with the dark jaggery and peanuts. The mixed is well mixed and is then made into Ladoos to grab the famous dish of the state.

Agriculture
Paddy

Chhattisgarh has often been dubbed “Rice Bowl” of Central India, with the main crop being Paddy. Apart from paddy, cereals like maize, kodo-kutki and other small millets, pulses like tur and kulthi and oilseeds like Groundnut, Soybean, Niger and Sunflower are also grown.

Festival
Bastar Dussehra

A festival that celebrates the power of the goddess Danteswari, who is revered by all the tribes of Chhattisgarh. During Dussehra, people in Bastar organize special worship ceremonies at the Danteswari temple in Jagadalpur.

Dress
Dhoti and kurta - Saree

The traditional dress of Chhattisgarh is dhoti-kurta for men and saree for women. The dhoti is a rectangular piece of cloth that is tied around the waist and legs, while the kurta is a long shirt that is worn over the dhoti.

Music
Folk songs

Some of the most popular folk songs of Chhattisgarh include the Sohar, Bihav, Sua, Goura, Khuduwa, Pathoni, Faag, Sawnahi, Cher Chera, Dohe, Bhojali, Dhankul, Loriya, Fugdi, and Kau Mau. The Bhojali is a notable folk song that showcases the state's rich cultural heritage and history

Dance
Folk dances

Some of the most popular folk dances of Chhattisgarh include the Raut Nacha, Panthi, and Soowa. The Cherchera dance is performed during the Cherchera festival, which is celebrated on the full moon night of the Paush Hindu calendar month.

Architecture
Kawardha Palace

Designed and built by Maharajah Dharamraj Singh between the period 1936-39 AD, using Italian white marble and stone, the amazing place is surrounded by greenery and scenic hills The architecture is a blend of Italian, Mogul and Colonial styles and the blending of different styles makes it more impressive.
