Maharashtra

Maharashtra


Chief Minister-Eknath Shinde

Eknath Sambhaji Shinde is an Indian politician who is serving as the 20th and current Chief Minister of Maharashtra since 30 June 2022.

Governor-C. P. Radhakrishnan

C. P. Radhakrishnan is an Indian politician who is serving 24th and current Governor of Maharashtra since 31 July 2024.

Culture and Heritage


Food
Vada pav

Vada pav, alternatively spelt wada pao, is a vegetarian fast food dish native to the Indian state of Maharashtra.

Agriculture
Mangoes

The Alphonso mango, also known as 'Hapus' in Maharashtra, is often hailed as the king of mangoes in India for its unparalleled sweetness, rich flavour, and creamy texture.

Festival
Gudi Padwa

Gudi Padwa is a major festival in Maharashtra that marks the beginning of the new year and harvest season. It's celebrated on the first day of the Hindu month of Chaitra, which usually falls in March or April.

Dress
Dhoti and Kurta Saree

The traditional dress for women is the Saree, which is draped around the body and worn with a blouse and petticoat. Men wear the Dhoti and Kurta, which is a long tunic worn over the Dhoti.

Music
Powada

Powada is a traditional Marathi ballad that celebrates and glorifies the deeds of popular leaders and folk figures. The word "Powada" literally means "to glorify".

Dance
Lavani

Lavani is a traditional folk dance and music form from Maharashtra, India. It's known for its powerful rhythm and is performed by women wearing nine-yard sarees, makeup, and ghunghroos.

Architecture
Wada

Wada architecture is a testament to meticulous craftsmanship and construction. Basalt or granite stones form the sturdy plinth, while thick load-bearing brick masonry builds the walls.
