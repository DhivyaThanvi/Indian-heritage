TamilNadu


Chief Minister-M K Stalin

Hon'ble Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu Thiru M.K.Stalin is the Head of the elected Government and heads the Council of Ministers. The Chief Justice, Madras High Court is the Head of Judiciary.

Governor-R. N. Ravi

Ravindra Narayana Ravi (born 3 April 1952)is an Indian politician and former bureaucrat serving as the current governor of Tamil Nadu.

Culture and Heritage


Food
Idlis Dosai

Several varieties like saada dosai, kal dosai, muttai dosai, neer dosai, rava dosai, raagi dosai and paasi paruppu dosai are prominently available in Tamil Nadu. Vadai is a crispy donut shaped spicy dish usually served along with idlis.

Agriculture
Rice

Paddy is Tamil Nadu's most important crop, and it comes in three varieties: Kuruvali, Thaladi, and Samba, which vary from season to season.

Festival
Pongal

Pongal is a four-day harvest festival celebrated by the Tamil community in Tamil Nadu and other parts of South India. It's dedicated to the Sun God, and is celebrated to thank the Sun, Mother Nature, and the farm animals that contribute to a bountiful harvest.

Dress
Vesti Saree

Costumes of Tamil Nadu - Men of this state put on a Dhoti or Lungi with a shirt and Angavastra. Women traditionally drape themselves in a sari. The Tamil way of draping a sari involves wrapping the pall around the waist. Younger girls wear a half-sari which is a skirt and blouse with a dupatta to drape around.

Music
Urumee mellam

The urumee mellam also remains as one of the more popular forms of folk music in rural Tamil Nadu and the ensemble is most often played with an urumee and the nadaswaram as the instrument of choice. The rural hill tribes of Tamil Nadu each have their own folk traditions.

Dance
Bharatanatyam

A popular classical dance form that originated in Tamil Nadu. It's known for its complex footwork, facial expressions, hand gestures, and storytelling.

Architecture
Dravidian architecture

The traditional architecture of Tamil Nadu is known as Dravidian architecture. It is known for its intricate, grand, and detailed design, and is a blend of Dravidian and Islamic styles.
