Andra Pradesh

Andra Pradesh


Chief Minister-Nara Chandrababu Naidu

Nara Chandrababu Naidu commonly known as CBN,is an Indian politician who is currently serving as the 13th Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh

Governor-S. Abdul Nazeer

He is a former of the Supreme Court of India and a former judge of the Karnataka High Court and also the Governor of Andhra Pradesh on 12 February 2023.

Culture and Heritage


Food
Pootharekulu

Pootharekulu is a popular Indian sweet from the Andhra Pradesh state of south-east India. The sweet is wrapped in a wafer-thin rice starch layer resembling paper and is stuffed with sugar, dry fruits and nuts.

Agriculture
Rice

Andhra Pradesh, a southern state in India, produces about 130 lakh tonnes of paddy annually, and contributes around 11 million metric tons of rice to the country's total production each year.

Festival
Ugadi

The word "Ugadi" is a combination of the words "Yuga" (age) and "Adi" (beginning). According to Hindu mythology, Lord Brahma created the world on this day. .

Dress
Dhoti and kurta

For men, Andhra Pradesh traditional dress is a dhoti and kurta. They also wear lungis and shirts. Andhra Pradesh traditional dress was dhotis for women as well prior to the 14th century .

Music
Folk

Andhra Pradesh is rich in folk music forms, including the narrative styles of 'Burrakatha' and 'Oggu Katha', the devotional 'Harikatha', 'Jangam Katha' which is performed by wandering minstrels, and 'Lambadi', the dance music of the Banjara community

Dance
Kuchipudi

A classical dance that originated in the state and is considered one of India's nine classical dance styles. The name comes from the place of origin, kuchelapuram, and the dance combines pure dance with drama and expression

Architecture
Dravidian style

One of the most prominent architectural styles in Andhra Pradesh is the Dravidian style, Another important architectural style in Andhra Pradesh is the Kakatiya style, which developed during the rule of the Kakatiya dynasty between the 12th and 14th centuries.
